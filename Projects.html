ANDROID HEART RATE MONITOR
(In progress)

I am currently in the progress of creating a Heart Rate monitor in Java for Android phones. It will use JavaCV to access the camera, which users will press their fingers against. It will then convert the photo to grayscale, then measure the time between pulses that will be visible. There has been a lot of research on this technology, so I plan on creating a heart rate monitor soon in the summer.
